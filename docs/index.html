WELCOME, SPELLOMANCER!

Spellomancer is a competitive game combining the brains of a word game with the brawn of a 2D action platformer. Each match takes place in a magical garden where you and your team attempt to harvest letters growing like crops. But beware! The opposing team is out for the best letters as well. You’ll need to fend them off with an arsenal of ridiculous weaponry and good, ol’ fashioned teamwork to get the best ones.

Carry all the letters you can back to your base and conspire with your team to spell the longest, highest scoring words in your giant spellbook. Pull the magic lever and presto! Points earned! The team with the highest score by the end of the round wins.

In Spellomancer, it takes both smarts and skills to emerge victorious. Do you have the magic touch?
